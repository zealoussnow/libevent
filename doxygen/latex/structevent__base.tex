\subsection{Detailed Description}
Structure to hold information and state for a Libevent dispatch loop. The \doxyref{event\_\-base}{p.}{structevent__base} lies at the center of Libevent; every application will have one. It keeps track of all pending and active events, and notifies your application of the active ones.
